About

Long ago a powerful warlord was defeated in battle. Too angry to die and bound to the earth by the atrocities he comitted, his dying soul shattered and wove into the fabric of reality creating a new dimensional plane now known as the Spirit Plane. Lost souls who cant find thier way to the afterlife embed into this plane of existance and become phantom spirits. Throughout the years, generation after generation were born with Phantom Marking. Awakinging these marking granted them Spirit Sabers and the eyes of the Phantom spirits which granted them vision into the spirit plane. These generations we tasked with guiding spirits down to the afterlife through any means necessary.
